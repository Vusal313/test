Create and Sell Your NFTs

1. Set up your wallet

Connect your preferred wallet to our marketplace by clicking the NFT Marketplace in the top right corner.

2. Create Your Collection

Create your collection with social links, a description, profile and banner images, and a secondary sales fee.

3. Add Your NFTs

Upload your work (image, video, audio, or 3D art), add a title and description, and customize your NFTs with properties and stats.

4. List Them For Sale

Choose between auctions, fixed-price listings, and declining-price listings. You choose how you want to sell your NFTs!
